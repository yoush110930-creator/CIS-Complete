Our History

Founded in 1985, Cebu International School has grown from a small community school to a leading educational institution in the region. Our journey is marked by a commitment to academic excellence, fostering a global perspective, and nurturing well-rounded individuals.

Accreditation

Cebu International School is fully accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges (WASC), ensuring our programs meet rigorous international standards. This accreditation reflects our dedication to continuous improvement and providing a high-quality education.
